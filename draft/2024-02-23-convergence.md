## pointwise convergence

${ f_n \to f }$

$$\lim_{n \to \infty} f_{n}(x) = f(x) $$
for all ${ x \in X }$.

## uniform convergence

${ f_{n} \rightrightarrows f}$

$$ \forall x \in X, \forall \varepsilon>0,\exists N\in\mathbb{N} (n\ge N \to \lvert f_{n}(x)-f(x) \rvert < \varepsilon)  $$
