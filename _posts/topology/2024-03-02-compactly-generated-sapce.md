## 정의

### k-closed sets

${ A \subseteq X }$ is **${ k }$-closed** if ${ u^{-1}(A) }$ is closed in ${ K }$ for every compact Hausdorff space ${ K }$ and define

$$ \mathbb{k}X=\{ k\mbox{-closed subsets of } X \} $$
