${ d: X\times X \to [0,\infty) }$
- ${ d(x,y)=0 \mbox{ iff } x=y }$
- ${ d(x,y) = d(y,x) }$ for all ${ x,y \in X }$
- ${ d(x,y) \le d(x,z) + d(z,y) }$ for all ${ x,y,z \in X }$
