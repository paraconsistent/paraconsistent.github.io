만약 self-adjoint operator ${ P }$가

$${ \mathrm{Spec}(P) \subset \left\{ 0,1 \right\} }$$

를 만족시키면,

$$ \langle Pu,Pu \rangle = \langle P^{2}u,u \rangle =\langle Pu,u \rangle $$

확률 1 iff ${ Pu=u }$
